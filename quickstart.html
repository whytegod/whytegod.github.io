Quickstart

This guide shows how to use the Whytegod Protocol to create and verify a permanent record in minutes.

Step 1 — Prepare Your Data

Choose the text or data you want to preserve. This could be a document, statement, log, or note.

Step 2 — Generate a Record ID

Go to the Hash Generator and paste your data. The output is your Record ID.

  • Keep the original data safe
  • The Record ID is public

Step 3 — Anchor the Record

Publish the Record ID to an immutable public system. This establishes existence at a specific time.

  • Blockchain transactions
  • Public timestamp services
  • Append-only public logs

Step 4 — Save the Reference

Store the transaction ID or anchor reference. This links your Record ID to a public timestamp.

Step 5 — Verify Anytime

Use the Verification Tool to confirm integrity. Matching hashes prove the record is unchanged.

Whytegod never stores your data. Verification remains possible as long as hashing standards exist.
