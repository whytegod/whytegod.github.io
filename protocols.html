WHYTEGOD Protocols Manifesto

Protocols

WHYTEGOD observes and records verifiable on-chain state changes across blockchain protocols. This page lists observed protocols and the standard used to document their history.

State Change Standard

A state change is any on-chain action that permanently alters a protocol’s control, code, or operational behavior.

  • Authority & control changes
  • Contract upgrades
  • Governance executions
  • Parameter modifications
  • Permission & access changes

Off-chain activity, price movement, narratives, and interpretation are explicitly excluded.
