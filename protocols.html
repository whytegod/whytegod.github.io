Protocol Layer

1. Supply Discipline

Whytegod operates on a fixed and immutable supply. No minting, no inflation, and no discretionary issuance. All calculations are deterministic and verifiable by any node.

2. Record Anchoring

Records are anchored using cryptographic commitments. Only proofs are stored on-chain, never raw data. This preserves privacy while ensuring permanence and verifiability.

3. Privacy by Absence

The protocol avoids identity entirely. There are no accounts, profiles, usernames, or metadata trails. Validation relies on mathematics, not trust.

4. Lifecycle Rules

Protocol behavior responds organically to usage demand. Fees and anchoring costs reflect network activity, reinforcing long-term value without speculative manipulation.

5. Internal & External Utility

Native units are used for internal anchoring operations and external integrations, enabling ecosystem growth without compromising protocol neutrality.

6. Threat Model

The system is designed to resist censorship, replay attacks, data forgery, and identity correlation. There are no privileged actors or upgrade keys.
