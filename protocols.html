Manifesto Protocols Use Cases Whitepaper

Protocols

WHYTEGOD observes decentralized protocols and records verifiable on-chain state changes.

Scope of Observation

WHYTEGOD does not attempt to cover all systems. Protocols are included based on on-chain verifiability, public accessibility, and clear authority boundaries.

Observation is passive. No interaction, execution, or participation occurs.

Observed Protocol Types

  • Layer 1 and Layer 2 networks
  • Governance frameworks and DAOs
  • Treasury and asset management contracts
  • Upgradeable smart contract systems
  • Core infrastructure protocols

Recorded State Changes

WHYTEGOD records changes that alter the effective behavior, authority, or configuration of a protocol.

  • Governance proposal execution
  • Permission and role changes
  • Parameter updates
  • Contract upgrades or replacements
  • Treasury movements initiated by protocol authority

Verification

Each record is linked to an on-chain transaction and timestamped at the moment the state change occurs.

Records reference primary sources only. Off-chain commentary, interpretation, or inference is excluded by design.

Explicit Exclusions

  • No market data or price information
  • No risk assessments
  • No forecasts or recommendations
  • No scoring or rankings
  • No incentives or affiliations

Protocols change through execution, not opinion.
