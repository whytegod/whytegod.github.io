Research Roadmap

A phased, research-first approach to understanding decentralized financial coordination.

Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1–3)

The foundation phase focuses on clarity, observation, and technical understanding.

  • Study core DeFi protocols (Uniswap, Aave, MakerDAO)
  • Document protocol mechanics and risk surfaces
  • Analyze governance structures and incentives
  • Publish neutral, educational research articles

Phase 2: Integration Research (Months 4–6)

This phase explores how users and DAOs interact with decentralized systems in practice.

  • Study wallet interactions and transaction flows
  • Map risk before, during, and after execution
  • Analyze cross-chain coordination challenges
  • Develop conceptual dashboards and visual models

Phase 3: Ecosystem Participation (Months 7–9)

Focus shifts toward collaboration without control or custody.

  • Release open research frameworks
  • Publish APIs or SDK concepts (non-custodial)
  • Engage DAO communities for feedback
  • Host open research discussions and reviews

Phase 4: Public Infrastructure (Months 10–12)

The final phase emphasizes sustainability, openness, and long-term credibility.

  • Formalize documentation and standards
  • Introduce multilingual research resources
  • Enable forkable, community-driven tools
  • Position WHYTEGOD as neutral public infrastructure

Guiding Principles

  • Research before building
  • Neutrality over influence
  • Transparency over optimization
  • Sovereignty over custody

WHYTEGOD evolves through understanding, not speculation or pressure.
