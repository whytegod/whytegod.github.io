Governance Principles

WHYTGOD is built on the principle that financial infrastructure must be neutral, transparent, and accountable.

Core Principles

  • No custody of user funds
  • No preferential routing
  • No hidden incentives
  • No opaque decision-making

Separation of Powers

WHYTGOD separates:

  • Policy definition (Open Financial Network)
  • Execution infrastructure (Whytegod Gateway)
  • Public communication (WHYTGOD)

Neutrality Commitment

WHYTGOD does not compete with banks, wallets, or payment providers. It exists to enable fair and auditable interoperability.

Accountability

All infrastructure decisions are documented. All routing logic is policy-driven. All execution layers are auditable.
