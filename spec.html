Whytegod Protocol Specification

This document defines the immutable monetary, cryptographic, and validation rules governing the Whytegod network.

Home Protocols Records

Monetary Supply

Whytegod operates under a permanently fixed maximum supply. No mechanism exists to increase supply after genesis.

  • Total Supply: 21,000,000 WHYTEGOD
  • Supply Cap: Enforced at protocol level
  • Emergency Minting: None
  • Governance Override: Not permitted

Base Denomination (Wert)

The smallest indivisible unit of Whytegod is called Wert. Wert is a neutral economic measurement unit derived from value theory, not branding or folklore.

  • 1 WHYTEGOD = 100,000,000 Wert
  • Wert cannot be subdivided
  • All protocol calculations occur in Wert

Issuance Model

Whytegod issuance follows a deterministic decay schedule. Emission decreases over time until reaching zero.

  • No perpetual inflation
  • No reward randomness
  • Issuance ends permanently

Validation Mechanism

The network uses Proof of Integrity, a validation model that rewards correctness rather than wealth or energy consumption.

  • Validators must maintain continuous uptime
  • Incorrect state transitions are penalized
  • Repeated faults lead to slashing and removal

Privacy Model

Privacy is enforced at the protocol level. Transactions obscure sender, receiver, and amount by default.

  • No opt-in privacy
  • No metadata leakage
  • No address reuse visibility

Governance Constraints

Whytegod does not use traditional on-chain governance. Core monetary rules cannot be altered by voting or authority.

  • No voting on supply
  • No leadership keys
  • No privileged actors

Finality & Immutability

Once confirmed, transactions are final. Historical records cannot be rewritten, censored, or reordered.
