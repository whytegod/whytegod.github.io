Manifesto

Reality first. Narrative later.

Reality first.

Most people react to narratives.
Narratives come after decisions.

Capital moves first.
Control changes first.
Stories follow.

WHYTEGOD exists to record what actually changes — not how people feel about it.

The Problem

Protocols evolve constantly. Permissions change. Parameters shift. Governance executes. Capital reallocates.

These changes are fragmented, forgotten, or rewritten. Without a clear record of state, responsibility disappears.

The Principle

Interpretation is a liability.

WHYTEGOD does not score, predict, rank, market, or recommend. We separate observation from opinion.

What We Do

WHYTEGOD is a protocol observatory.

We document verifiable on-chain state changes, anchored to timestamps, transactions, and sources.

The goal is continuity of truth — not persuasion.

What We Refuse to Do

  • No custody of funds
  • No execution of transactions
  • No incentives or token issuance
  • No influence over decisions
  • No narrative engineering

Position

WHYTEGOD is not a financial product. It does not provide advice. It does not act on behalf of users.

Participation is voluntary. Interpretation remains the responsibility of the reader.

Final

State does not lie.
Stories do.
