WHYTEGOD Manifesto

Manifesto

Reality first.

Most people react to narratives. Narratives come after decisions.

Capital moves first. Control changes first. Stories follow.

WHYTEGOD exists to record what actually changes — not how people feel about it.

The Problem

Protocols evolve constantly: permissions change, parameters shift, governance executes, capital reallocates.

These changes are fragmented, forgotten, or rewritten. Without clear state history, responsibility disappears.

Our Principle

Interpretation is a liability. WHYTEGOD does not score, predict, rank, or recommend.

What We Do

WHYTEGOD is a protocol observatory. We record verifiable on-chain state changes with timestamps and sources.

What We Refuse to Do

  • No custody
  • No execution
  • No incentives
  • No influence

Final

State does not lie. Stories do.
