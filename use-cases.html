Real-World Use Cases

The Whytegod Protocol is designed for situations where proof, permanence, and neutrality matter more than control.

Legal & Evidence Preservation

  • Timestamp legal documents
  • Preserve evidence integrity
  • Prove document existence without disclosure
  • Support dispute resolution

Intellectual Property & Research

  • Prove prior art
  • Anchor research drafts
  • Establish invention timelines
  • Protect academic integrity

Public Records & Transparency

  • Publish immutable public statements
  • Archive organizational commitments
  • Prevent silent document changes
  • Enable independent verification

Journalism & Whistleblowing

  • Prove possession of information at a time
  • Protect sources
  • Enable delayed disclosure
  • Resist censorship

Software & System Logs

  • Anchor system states
  • Prove log integrity
  • Detect tampering
  • Support audits

What Whytegod Does Not Decide

  • Truth or falsehood
  • Ownership or authority
  • Legal interpretation
  • Access permissions

Whytegod provides proof of existence and integrity — interpretation is left to humans, courts, and institutions.
