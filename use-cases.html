Manifesto Protocols Use Cases Whitepaper

Use Cases

WHYTEGOD supports users who require factual, verifiable records of protocol state changes.

Overview

WHYTEGOD is not a tool for speculation or decision-making. It exists to provide reliable historical context for decentralized systems.

Use cases emerge where accountability, traceability, and verification matter.

Researchers & Analysts

Researchers use WHYTEGOD to study how protocols evolve over time without relying on narratives, dashboards, or retrospective explanations.

  • Tracking governance execution patterns
  • Analyzing protocol upgrade frequency
  • Studying authority and permission changes

Governance Participants

Governance actors reference WHYTEGOD to verify what has already been executed on-chain, independent of proposals, discussions, or intent.

  • Confirming executed proposals
  • Reviewing historical parameter changes
  • Auditing authority transitions

Auditors & Compliance Teams

Auditors and compliance teams require primary-source evidence of system behavior.

WHYTEGOD provides time-ordered records linked directly to on-chain transactions.

Protocol Builders

Builders use WHYTEGOD to understand how existing systems manage upgrades, governance authority, and control boundaries.

  • Designing upgrade-safe architectures
  • Evaluating governance risk surfaces
  • Learning from historical execution patterns

Use cases emerge from facts, not persuasion.
