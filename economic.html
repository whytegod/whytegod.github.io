Economic Model

The Whytegod Protocol uses WGD as a minimal economic utility to prevent abuse and support long-term sustainability.

Purpose of WGD

  • Anti-spam mechanism
  • Anchoring cost unit
  • Neutral demand signal

What WGD Is Not

  • Not a governance token
  • Not an ownership claim
  • Not a dividend or profit right
  • Not a promise of appreciation

Anchoring Cost

Anchoring a record requires a fixed amount of WGD. The token is consumed as part of the anchoring process.

  • Cost introduces friction
  • Prevents protocol abuse
  • Encourages meaningful records

Supply Philosophy

The protocol does not dynamically manage price or supply. Market dynamics are external to the protocol.

Whytegod defines usage rules only. Economic interpretation is external and voluntary.
