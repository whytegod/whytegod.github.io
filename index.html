WHYTEGOD

A verifiable protocol observatory and scarce economic network.

WHYTEGOD records objective on-chain state changes and secures them with a fixed-supply economic system. No narratives. No opinions. Only verifiable protocol reality.

How It Works

  • Observe: Protocol state changes are submitted on-chain.
  • Bond: Submissions require WGD bonds to prevent manipulation.
  • Verify: Validators stake WGD to attest accuracy.
  • Record: Verified changes become immutable protocol records.

Truth is enforced economically. Falsehood is costly.

Economic Design

WHYTEGOD operates with a fixed maximum supply of 21,000,000 WGD.

The smallest accounting unit is wert.
1 WGD = 100,000,000 wert

  • No inflation
  • No discretionary minting
  • No governance-controlled supply

WGD is required for staking, record submission, verification, and priority protocol access.

Participate

WHYTEGOD distributes a finite portion of its supply to users who actively contribute to the protocol.

  • Validator staking
  • Record submission and verification
  • Usage-based airdrop participation

No free money. No inflation. Contribution is required.

WHYTEGOD combines fixed-supply discipline, economic enforcement, and protocol observability into a single, verifiable system.

WHYTEGOD is a fixed-supply protocol observatory.
Scarcity is enforced by code.
