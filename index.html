About WHYTEGOD

WHYTEGOD is an open financial network built on transparency, self-custody, and permissionless access.

The project explores decentralized finance, open protocols, and public infrastructure that empowers individuals without relying on custodians or intermediaries.

WHYTEGOD is a public-facing experiment focused on education, innovation, and open collaboration.
