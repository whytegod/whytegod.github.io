WHYTEGOD

AI-first, non-custodial peer-to-peer finance infrastructure.

WHYTEGOD is a product vision and interface layer for decentralized finance — focused on clarity, access, and trust minimization.

What WHYTEGOD Is

WHYTEGOD is an AI-assisted, non-custodial coordination layer designed to help users navigate peer-to-peer finance without surrendering control.

  • AI-assisted P2P finance interface
  • Non-custodial by design
  • User-first gateway to open financial protocols
  • Product expression of open financial principles

What WHYTEGOD Is Not

WHYTEGOD is not:

  • A bank
  • An exchange
  • A broker or dealer
  • An investment product
  • A fund or yield platform
  • A custodian of user assets

WHYTEGOD does not provide financial guarantees or promises of profit.

Relationship to Open Financial Network (OFN)

WHYTEGOD is aligned with, but independent from, the Open Financial Network (OFN).

OFN defines protocol-level standards. WHYTEGOD represents a product-facing implementation and philosophy.

This separation ensures neutrality, forkability, and long-term integrity.

Core Principles

  • Non-custodial by design — users retain full asset control
  • AI as assistant — clarity without opaque authority
  • Trust minimization — reduce reliance on intermediaries
  • Privacy by default — reputation without exposure
  • Forkability — exit over control

Status

This project represents intent and direction — not a deployed financial product.
