WHYTEGOD

AI-first, non-custodial coordination for open finance.

WHYTEGOD is a product-facing interface designed to help users navigate open financial systems without surrendering custody, control, or sovereignty.

What It Is

WHYTEGOD operates at the coordination layer — connecting users to open financial infrastructure while explicitly limiting its own authority.

It does not execute transactions, custody assets, or define protocol rules.

What It Is Not

WHYTEGOD is not a bank, exchange, custodian, broker, or investment product. It makes no promises of profit and provides no financial guarantees.

Standards Alignment

WHYTEGOD is aligned with open financial standards, including the Open Financial Network (OFN).

OFN defines protocol rules. WHYTEGOD provides a user-facing coordination surface. The two remain independent by design.

Status

Current status: Concept / Pre-implementation

This site documents intent, boundaries, and principles — not a live deployment.
