What WHYTEGOD Is

WHYTEGOD is a protocol observatory. We document confirmed, irreversible changes in blockchain protocol state — governance executions, contract upgrades, parameter shifts, and capital reconfiguration.

We do not predict markets. We do not rank protocols. We do not offer opinions.

We preserve state history so responsibility cannot disappear.

Why It Exists

Protocols evolve faster than narratives. By the time stories are written, control has already shifted.

Without a neutral state record, history becomes editable. WHYTEGOD exists to make protocol changes permanent, visible, and verifiable.

What We Do

  • Record on-chain state changes
  • Timestamp protocol evolution
  • Preserve execution history

What We Refuse

  • No custody
  • No execution
  • No incentives
  • No interpretation
