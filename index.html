What Is WHYTEGOD?

WHYTEGOD is an independent research initiative focused on clarity, neutrality, and education within decentralized finance and DAO governance. It does not custody funds, recommend trades, or influence decisions.

Core Principles

  • Neutral analysis
  • Non-custodial design
