What WHYTEGOD Is

WHYTEGOD is a protocol observatory. We document when decentralized systems change — governance execution, permission updates, parameter shifts, and capital reallocation.

We do not interpret. We do not rank. We preserve state history so responsibility cannot disappear.

Why It Exists

Protocols evolve faster than memory. Without durable records, narratives replace facts.

WHYTEGOD exists so state changes remain visible, verifiable, and permanent.
