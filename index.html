Whytegod Protocol

Whytegod is a fixed-supply protocol designed for permanence, neutrality, and privacy by absence. It enables verifiable records without exposing identities, mutable history, or centralized control.

The protocol borrows Bitcoin’s supply discipline and Monero’s privacy philosophy, while extending utility into record anchoring, verification, and long-term data integrity.
