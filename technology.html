WHYTEGOD Technology Paper

Overview

WHYTEGOD operates as a coordination layer rather than a centralized system. It does not custody assets, host funds, or enforce trades.

Architecture Philosophy

The system is designed to minimize trust by relying on decentralized primitives, peer verification, and protocol-enforced logic instead of human discretion.

AI Assistance Layer

Artificial intelligence is used as a guidance and coordination tool, helping users discover peers, reduce mistakes, and understand options without removing final decision-making authority.

Security Model

Security emerges from non-custodial design, absence of centralized attack vectors, and gradual evolution rather than rapid, fragile deployment.

Scalability

WHYTEGOD is asset-agnostic and chain-agnostic by design, allowing it to adapt to multiple settlement layers without protocol dependency.
