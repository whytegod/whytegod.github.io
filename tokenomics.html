Tokenomics

WGD exists to introduce cost, scarcity, and friction into record anchoring — nothing more.

Token Name

Whytegod (WGD)

Purpose of WGD

  • Prevent spam anchoring
  • Introduce economic weight to records
  • Make anchoring intentional and costly

What WGD Is NOT

  • Not an investment
  • Not a security
  • Not governance power
  • Not ownership or equity

Token Usage

WGD is consumed when anchoring a WRID into a public immutable system.

  • Anchoring requires WGD
  • Verification is always free
  • Reading records is permissionless

Economic Logic

A record with cost carries intent. A record without cost carries noise.

WGD ensures that anchoring is deliberate, not casual.

Supply Philosophy

  • Finite or controlled issuance
  • No inflation promises
  • No yield or rewards

Burn / Consumption

WGD used for anchoring may be:

  • Burned
  • Locked
  • Redirected to protocol costs

Exact mechanics are external to the protocol.

WGD defines access cost — not authority. The protocol remains neutral forever.
